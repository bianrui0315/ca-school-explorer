# California School Explorer v0.5.0

Release 0.5.0 adds a dedicated Teaching & resources workspace backed by six official CDE snapshots and a new canonical school-resource fact model.

## What families can do

- Compare teacher experience, teacher preparation and placement, class size, student support FTE, and pupils per academic counselor for up to five public schools.
- Switch among the three published class-size years and three teacher-preparation years.
- See each section's actual reporting year instead of combining differently dated records.
- Search, add, and remove schools without leaving the workspace.
- See `Not reported` for blank or unusable source values rather than a reconstructed zero.

## Data and delivery

- Six immutable source manifests pin official URLs, headers, byte sizes, record counts, encodings, release dates, and SHA-256 digests.
- Migration `0009_teaching_resources.sql` adds source definitions, resource metric definitions, provenance-preserving facts, indexes, and a current-observation view.
- The canonical database now contains 26 imported snapshots, 3,962,208 outcome facts, 432,598 school-resource facts, and 9,946 current public-school profiles.
- The static public bundle adds 428,035 lazily loaded resource observations across 441 bounded shards.
- Resource shards load only on the Teaching & resources route; PostgreSQL remains private and is not used for visitor requests.

## Known limitations

- Staff experience is 2025–26, class size and student support are 2024–25, and teacher preparation currently ends in 2023–24.
- Pupils-per-counselor coverage is 58.4%, materially lower than the other measures.
- Resource measures are descriptive context, not a rating, ranking, or causal explanation of outcomes.
- SARC completeness and accuracy can reflect local educational agency reporting.
