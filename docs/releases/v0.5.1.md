# California School Explorer v0.5.1

Release 0.5.1 adds a shareable, evidence-first profile for every current public school in the versioned directory.

## What families can do

- Open a stable `/school/{CDS-code}` URL from a comparison card or shared link.
- Read school identity, enrollment, reported staffing, latest outcome evidence, actual three-year trends, subgroup context, teaching resources, and location details in one report.
- Change the detailed outcome indicator and student group without losing the profile URL.
- Add the school to comparison, open the full teaching comparison, copy the profile link, or find another school.
- Use the report on desktop, mobile, or in print without a rating, rank, or AI-generated recommendation.

## Data and delivery

- Profiles reuse the existing outcome, resource, district, reference, directory, and coordinate shards; no duplicate school-profile dataset or visitor database was added.
- Direct profile visits initially load only the statewide index plus the selected school's bounded outcome and resource shards.
- Page titles and descriptions update for the selected school, while unknown CDS codes receive a recoverable search state.

## Verification

- Route, direct-load, comparison handoff, unknown-CDS, metric, subgroup, teaching-resource, and responsive interaction tests cover the new flow.
- The release retains source reporting years, suppression, reliability, and missing-value behavior from the canonical public-data contract.

## Known limitations

- Profiles are client-rendered static routes; dedicated server-rendered social preview images remain future work.
- Reporting years differ by source, and individual schools may lack measures because of grade eligibility, suppression, or local reporting completeness.
- Published coordinates and district context do not identify an assigned attendance school or guarantee enrollment eligibility.
