# California School Explorer v0.4.0

Release date: 2026-07-15

## Added

- Three checksum-pinned CDE College/Career Indicator snapshots covering 2022–23 through 2024–25.
- The checksum-pinned 2022–23 CDE 12-month College-Going Rate file, with all-completer filtering and subgroup support.
- Distinct comparison metrics for CCI Prepared and college-going within 12 months; A–G completion remains separately defined.
- CCI in the eight-axis current-year profile, editable composite, and high-school location evidence model.
- Metric-specific denominator, release-lag, methodology-change, and National Student Clearinghouse privacy-block cautions.
- Exact-address district jurisdiction from the authoritative CDE 2025–26 district-area service, including overlapping elementary and high school districts where applicable.
- Bounded, no-store Worker and browser clients for official district-area lookup, with tests for input validation, privacy-preserving POST requests, and rendered caveats.

## Data

- Canonical PostgreSQL facts: 3,962,208 across nine metrics.
- Imported snapshots: 20, including 19 outcome snapshots and one current school-profile snapshot.
- Public browser observations: 2,878,340 across 9,946 schools and 1,023 district baselines.
- College-going remains at its latest available 2022–23 source year and is excluded from current-year composite and location scores.

## Known limitations

- A CDE district-area match identifies district jurisdiction, not an assigned school. School attendance zones require current official local district data.
- CCI qualifying measures can change across Dashboard releases, so longitudinal movement is not necessarily a pure outcome change.
- College-going may undercount FERPA-blocked National Student Clearinghouse records and uses a different population from four-year graduation.
- Similar-context comparisons, statewide school attendance boundaries, private-school profiles, and additional postsecondary years remain planned.
