# California School Explorer v0.6.0

Release 0.6.0 turns the existing location, comparison, and trend tools into one guided Family Decision Brief.

## What families can do

- Enter a California address, city, or ZIP and retain the selected grade, radius, public-school type, evidence threshold, and evidence priorities.
- Select up to three eligible Area Explorer matches without changing the separate five-school comparison workspace.
- Open one concise report containing distance, district, grade span, school type, evidence coverage, the latest ELA, mathematics, chronic absence, suspension, graduation, dropout, A–G, and College/Career values, and compact three-year directions.
- Read deterministic statements about each school's strongest normalized signal, the widest comparable difference, and latest missing-data gaps.
- Open each full school profile, continue to the full comparison, return to the editable location search, copy a reproducible link, or print the brief.
- Try the complete flow immediately with a Porter Ranch high-school sample backed by the same public records as any user-created brief.

## Interpretation boundaries

- The brief evidence score is an experimental summary of available weighted evidence, not a rank or official rating.
- Deterministic templates summarize cited values; no runtime AI interprets, predicts, estimates, or rewrites school outcomes.
- Missing, suppressed, small-sample, and differently dated values remain visible and are never reconstructed.
- Nearby does not mean assigned or eligible. Families must confirm attendance boundaries, enrollment rules, programs, transportation, and admissions with the school or district.

## Verification

- Share-state parsing rejects invalid location settings, malformed CDS codes, duplicate IDs, and selections larger than three schools.
- Unit tests cover evidence selection, material differences, missing-data statements, trend direction, Area Explorer selection, direct brief loading, and edit-search restoration.
- Desktop, mobile, print, direct-link, sample, profile, and comparison handoffs are verified before deployment.
