# California School Explorer v0.1.0

This is the first public, real-data release of California School Explorer.

## What is included

- Searchable profiles for 9,946 California public schools.
- 1,017 same-district comparison baselines.
- 920,813 school and district observations derived from six official CDE snapshots.
- Seven indicators: ELA and mathematics distance from standard, chronic absenteeism, suspension, four-year graduation, A–G completion, and four-year dropout.
- 31 student-group lenses, including English learners, students with disabilities, racial and ethnic groups, and socioeconomically disadvantaged students.
- Side-by-side comparison for up to five schools with exact values, sample sizes, reliability labels, suppression notices, and source metadata.
- A static, versioned public data contract that deploys without a runtime database or API.

## Data years

- Outcomes: 2024–25.
- Public-school profiles: 2025–26.

Because the first release contains one outcome year, the trend chart currently displays one point per series. Historical trends are the next major data milestone.

## Important interpretation notes

- A–G completion is not the broader California Dashboard College/Career Indicator.
- Suppressed values remain unavailable and are never inferred.
- Small samples are visibly labeled.
- The site does not assign a composite score or rank schools.
- The project is independent and is not produced or endorsed by the California Department of Education.

Raw CDE source files are not included or relicensed. Published records are selected factual derivatives with source attribution and immutable snapshot metadata. Formal source-specific permission review remains an open governance item.
